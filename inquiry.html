II Chapter Two — The Journey

From inquiry to wedding morning, set out plainly.

Every booking moves through four calm, considered steps — so the morning of your celebration feels light, prepared, and entirely yours.

  1. N° 01
    Inquiry

    Send the details

    Share your date, ready time, location, party size, services, and any reference images. The form takes about five minutes — the more detail, the better the response.

    Reply within 24-48 hours
  2. N° 02
    Consultation

    Confirm the direction

    Dwija reviews your beauty direction, skin needs, hair texture, timeline, and quote fit. Optional video or in-person consult to align on the day-of mood and finish.

    Quote & availability confirmed
  3. N° 03
    Trial

    Preview the finish

    An optional in-person preview to test custom tones, refine complexion direction, verify hair holds, and lock the day-of plan so nothing feels rushed on the wedding morning.

    Day-of plan secured
  4. N° 04
    Wedding day

    On-location artistry

    Dwija arrives on time, sets up calmly, and works to a composed schedule. Touch-up kit, photo-ready finish, lashes, and steady support through the morning.

    Composed, camera-ready
III Chapter Three — Common Questions

Answers before you send.

Quick answers to the most common questions about booking, travel, trials, and bridal party support.

Most inquiries receive a response within 24-48 hours. Wedding-season weekends can take a touch longer — if it's been more than three business days, please send a follow-up to hello@definedbydwija.com.

A rough idea is enough at the inquiry stage. Once we confirm, Dwija will help you map a calm ready-by time and any earlier starts for the bridal party so everyone is finished without rushing.

Yes — bridal hair and bridal makeup can be booked individually. Note that paired services often look more cohesive in photos, and the trial is more useful when both are tested together.

Travel fees are quoted individually based on your getting-ready location — distance, accommodation needs, and timing all factor in. After your inquiry, Dwija will share a travel estimate alongside your tailored package quote. Greater Vancouver (Burnaby, Vancouver, Surrey, Richmond, Coquitlam, North Vancouver), surrounding cities, and destination dates are all available.
